Our services

Everything your water needs

We specialize in a complete range of water treatment solutions designed to improve water quality efficiently and reliably for homes and businesses across Indiana.

Free Water Testing

A complimentary in-home test that pinpoints hardness, iron, chlorine, pH, and contaminants. No cost, no obligation — just clear answers so you know exactly what you're dealing with.

Water Softeners

Eliminate hard-water scale that damages plumbing and appliances. Enjoy softer skin, brighter laundry, spotless dishes, and lower soap usage — with systems that last for decades.

Whole-Home Filtration

Remove sediment, chlorine taste and odor, and harmful contaminants at the point of entry — so every faucet, shower, and appliance in your home gets cleaner water.

Reverse Osmosis Drinking Water

Bottle-quality drinking water straight from your kitchen tap. Our RO systems strip out virtually all impurities for crisp, pure water for drinking, cooking, coffee, and ice.

UV Purification

Chemical-free ultraviolet disinfection that neutralizes bacteria, viruses, and cysts with no added taste or odor. Ideal for well water and rural water supplies.

Service, Maintenance & Repair

We offer a variety of services to help you maintain and repair your water treatment systems — keeping them running their best for years of dependable performance.
