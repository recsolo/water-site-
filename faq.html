Frequently asked questions

Everything you want to know

Is the water test really free?

Yes — completely free with no obligation. We come to your home, test your water on-site, and explain the results in plain language. Whether or not you decide to move forward with a system, there's no charge and no pressure.

What areas of Indiana do you serve?

We serve residential and commercial customers throughout the entire state of Indiana, operating out of our Indianapolis location at 5677 West 73rd Street.

What makes RainSoft systems different from competitors?

RainSoft systems are built to last for decades with premium components and engineering that far outlasts cheaper alternatives. As Indiana's exclusive RainSoft partner, we know these systems inside and out and can match the right system to your specific water and household.

How long does installation take?

Most installations are completed in a single visit, typically 2–4 hours depending on the system. Our certified technicians work cleanly and efficiently and will walk you through how everything works before they leave.

What contaminants can you treat?

We treat a wide range including hardness minerals (calcium and magnesium), iron, chlorine, chloramines, sediment, sulfur odor, bacteria, nitrates, heavy metals, and many other contaminants. The specific solution depends on what your water test reveals.

Do you service systems you didn't install?

Yes. In addition to servicing and maintaining the systems we install, we offer repair and maintenance services for most brands of water treatment equipment. Give us a call and we'll let you know how we can help.

Do you offer financing?

Please contact us directly for current financing options — we work to make clean water affordable for every household. Call us at 855-472-4676 or submit a contact form and we'll discuss the options available to you.

How do I know which system I need?

That's exactly what the free water test is for. We test your specific water, consider your household size, budget, and goals, and recommend only the system that actually fits your needs — never more.

Can I visit you in person?

Absolutely — we love our customers! Feel free to visit us at 5677 West 73rd Street, Indianapolis, IN during our business hours: Monday through Friday, 9:00am to 5:00pm.
